Imposter Fakeout - Privacy Policy

Your Data Stays on Your Device

All game data and user preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or process any of your information on remote servers.

Analytics & Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how players interact with the app and improve the experience. This includes app events (such as starting a game), feature usage patterns, game settings, and performance data. When you create a custom category, the topic name you enter is also collected to help us understand feature usage. We do not collect names, contact information, or other personally identifiable information. Analytics are processed by PostHog according to their privacy policy.

On-Device AI (Apple Intelligence)

When you use the AI word generation feature, your topic prompt is processed entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence. No data from this feature is sent to our servers or any third party.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

For premium features, we use RevenueCat to process payments and manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect transaction information and device identifiers necessary for subscription management. This data is processed according to RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for subscription management and PostHog for anonymous usage analytics. These services may collect certain information necessary for their operation. We do not use advertising networks or user tracking for marketing purposes.

Your Privacy Control

You always maintain full control of your data. If you delete the app, all associated data is removed from your device completely.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at apustovidko.dev@gmail.com.
