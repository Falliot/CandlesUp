Rock Identifier - Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Camera

When you scan rock and mineral photos, images are processed locally on your device and sent securely to OpenAPI for geological identification analysis. Images are not stored on our servers and are only used for the analysis you requested.

AI Analysis & Data Processing

We use AI to analyze rock and mineral photos and provide geological identification with confidence scoring. The image data is transmitted securely for processing according to their privacy policy. No personal information is included with these requests.

Local Data Storage

Your scan history, rock identification results, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share specific results.

Premium Subscriptions

For premium features and subscriptions, we use RevenueCat to process payments and manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect transaction information and device identifiers necessary for subscription management according to their privacy policy.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or track your behavior within the app. We respect your privacy and do not use any third-party analytics services.

Data Retention

Rock identification history is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual scans or clear all history at any time through the app. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

Third-Party Services

We use OpenAPI for AI geological analysis and RevenueCat for subscription management. We do not use advertising networks, social media trackers, or other third-party analytics services.

Feature Requests & Feedback

If you send feedback via email, only the information you voluntarily provide is collected. This information is used solely to improve the app and respond to your requests.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can view, delete, or export your scan history at any time. Contact us at apustovidko.dev@gmail.com for any privacy-related questions.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
