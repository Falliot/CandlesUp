Your Data Stays on Your Device

All your birthday data and user preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or process any of your information on remote servers.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage statistics, or analytics. There is no tracking of your activity within the app.

Contacts Access

When you choose to import birthdays from your contacts, this process happens entirely on your device. Contact information is never transmitted outside your device or stored elsewhere.

Notifications

Birthday reminders are scheduled locally using your device's notification system. We do not use remote push notifications or any server infrastructure.

No Third-Party Services

We don't integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services in our app.

Your Privacy Control

You always maintain full control of your data. If you delete the app, all associated data is removed from your device completely.
