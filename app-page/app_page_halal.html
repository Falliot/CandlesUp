HalalScan - App Details

App Overview

HalalScan is your smart, AI-powered food and ingredient checker. Instantly identify whether food is Halal, Haram, Mushbooh, or Unclassified using real-time camera scans, AI classification, and Islamic dietary guidance.

Core Scanning Features

  • Camera Integration – Scan ingredients live using your phone camera.
  • Quick & Detailed Modes – Choose between fast scans or in-depth analysis.
  • AI Classification – Halal, Haram, Mushbooh, or Unclassified with confidence scores.

AI-Powered Analysis

  • Islamic Guidance – Extract halal rulings and certification info.
  • Follow-Up Chat – Ask questions and explore Islamic reasoning.
  • Fun Mode – Lighthearted scan results, still respectful and informative.

Data & History

  • Local Scan History – Securely saved on your device.
  • Bookmarks & Export – Save or export important scans privately.

Premium Features

  • Unlimited Scans – No daily limits with premium.
  • Advanced Features – Full AI chat, fun mode, and deep analysis unlocked.
  • Smart Paywall – Trial included before upgrade prompt.

User Experience

  • Onboarding Flow – Guided intro to app features.
  • Accessibility – Full VoiceOver and dynamic type support.
  • Dark Mode & Localization – Multilingual and lighting-friendly.

Privacy & Security

  • Local-First – All data stays on your device.
  • No Tracking – No analytics, no surveillance.
  • Encrypted Communication – Secure interaction with OpenAI.
