Imposter Fakeout - App Details

App Overview

Imposter Fakeout is the ultimate party word-guessing game. One phone, up to 24 players — everyone gets a secret word, except the imposters. Discuss, bluff, and vote to find out who's faking it before they slip away undetected.

Key Features

  • One Phone, No Peeking – Pass the phone around so each player sees only their own role.
  • 50+ Categories – From Animals and Food to Movies, Sports, and more.
  • Custom Categories – Create your own word packs manually or let Apple Intelligence generate one instantly.
  • Up to 24 Players – Scales from a small group to a big party.
  • Name Your Players – Assign names to each player for a personalised experience.
  • 30+ Languages – Play anywhere in the world in your own language.

Game Modes

  • Classic – Everyone knows the word except the imposters. Find them before they fool you.
  • Chaos Mode – Roles are scrambled for maximum mayhem. Unlock by leaving a review.

Advanced Options

  • Mr. White – One player knows only the category, not the word.
  • Hint for Imposter – Give imposters the category name to help them blend in.
  • Imposters Know Each Other – Each imposter sees who their partners are during reveal.
  • Mystery Twist – Some rounds secretly mess with roles — expect the unexpected.
  • Timer – Add a countdown to keep discussions on pace.

Thoughtfully Designed

  • Modern Interface – Clean animations and smooth navigation built for iOS.
  • Dark Mode Support – Comfortable viewing in any lighting condition.
  • On-Device AI – Custom word generation via Apple Intelligence runs entirely on your device.
  • Privacy First – All game data stays on your device. No accounts required.
