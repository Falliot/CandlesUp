Dog Breed Identifier - App Details

App Overview

Dog Breed Identifier is your smart, AI-powered companion for identifying dog breeds from photos. Instantly discover breed information, care recommendations, health insights, and growth predictions using advanced AI technology and comprehensive breed databases.

Core Identification Features

  • Camera Integration – Capture or upload photos for instant breed analysis.
  • Multi-Pass AI Analysis – First classifies size/shape, then specific breed for enhanced accuracy.
  • Confidence Scoring – 0.0-1.0 reliability scale with specific thresholds for result accuracy.
  • Smart Photo Tips – Guidance for optimal photo capture and lighting.

Breed Information & Insights

  • Comprehensive Breed Profiles – Detailed information on temperament, characteristics, and history.
  • Care Recommendations – Personalized advice for exercise, grooming, and nutrition.
  • Health Insights – Common health concerns and preventive care tips.

Scan History & Management

  • Local Scan History – Securely saved on your device with SwiftData.

User Experience

  • Onboarding Flow – Guided introduction to app features and camera permissions.
  • Multi-Language Support – Available in English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Ukrainian, and Chinese.
  • Modern SwiftUI Interface – Clean, intuitive design optimized for iOS 17.6+.

Technical Features

  • AI-Powered – ChatGPT integration service for accurate breed detection.
  • Offline-First – Core functionality works without internet connection.
  • Camera Permissions – Smart permission handling with educational prompts.
  • Quick Actions – iOS shortcuts integration for faster access.

Feedback & Support

  • Feature Requests – WishKit integration for community-driven development.
  • Email Support – Built-in mail composition for user assistance.
  • Review Prompts – Smart timing for App Store review requests.
