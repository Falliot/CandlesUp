Rock Identifier - App Details

App Overview

Rock Identifier is your smart, AI-powered companion for identifying rocks and minerals from photos. Instantly discover geological information, formation details, collection advice, and comprehensive insights using advanced AI technology and extensive geological databases.

Core Identification Features

  • Camera Integration – Capture or upload photos for instant geological analysis.
  • Multi-Pass AI Analysis – First classifies rock type, then specific mineral identification for enhanced accuracy.
  • Confidence Scoring – 0.0-1.0 reliability scale with specific thresholds for result accuracy.
  • Smart Photo Tips – Guidance for optimal photo capture and lighting.
  • Formation Analysis – Identifies geological processes and formation environments.

Geological Information & Insights

  • Comprehensive Rock Profiles – Detailed information on physical properties, characteristics, and geological history.
  • Collection Recommendations – Expert advice for collecting, storing, and displaying rock specimens.
  • Formation Insights – Geological processes, age, and formation environments specific to each rock type.
  • Property Analysis – Understanding hardness, luster, crystal systems, and mineral composition.

Premium Features

  • Unlimited Scans – No daily limits on rock identification.
  • Formation Analysis – Detailed geological formation and age information.
  • Advanced Mineral Composition – Detailed mineral breakdowns and crystal structure analysis.
  • Market Value Estimations – Collector market value and rarity assessments.

Free users get 3 scans per day with basic rock information.

Scan History & Management

  • Local Scan History – Securely saved on your device with SwiftData.
  • Search & Filter – Easily find previous scans by rock type or date.
  • Export Options – Share results with geology enthusiasts or educators.

User Experience

  • Camera-First Design – Optimized for quick and easy photo capture.
  • Onboarding Flow – Guided introduction to app features and camera permissions.
  • English Language – Focused English-only interface for optimal geological terminology and accuracy.
  • Modern SwiftUI Interface – Clean, intuitive design optimized for iOS 17.6+.
  • Quick Actions – iOS shortcuts integration for faster access to scanning and feedback.

Technical Features

  • AI-Powered – OpenAPI service for accurate geological identification.
  • Privacy-First – All data stored locally, images processed securely.
  • Camera Permissions – Smart permission handling with educational prompts.
  • RevenueCat Integration – Seamless subscription management with remote paywall configuration.

Feedback & Support

  • Email Support – Built-in mail composition for user assistance.
  • Review Prompts – Smart timing for App Store review requests.
  • Identification Feedback – Help improve AI accuracy with geological result feedback.
