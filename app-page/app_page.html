CandlesUp - App Details

App Overview

CandlesUp is your all-in-one solution for tracking and celebrating the special days of your loved ones. With a beautiful, intuitive interface and powerful features, you’ll always be ready to celebrate.

Key Features

  • Effortless Tracking – Manually add birthdays or import them directly from your contacts.
  • Countdown Timer – See the exact time left until upcoming birthdays.
  • Personalized Experience – Set up your own birthday profile and track your countdown.
  • Rich Details – View age, Western & Chinese zodiac signs, and more.
  • Quick Actions – Manage contacts, notifications, and calendar views with a single tap.
  • Insights – Get to know your friends and their birthday analytics.

Celebrate with Ease

  • Gift Suggestions – Get region-specific gift ideas tailored to your location.
  • Instant Birthday Wishes – Send greetings via WhatsApp, Messages, Email, or calls.
  • Calendar Integration – Add birthdays with automatic yearly recurrence.
  • Custom Alerts – Set personalized reminders so you never miss a special day.

Privacy-Focused

  • 100% Private – All data stays on your device.
  • No Tracking – No analytics or third-party services.
  • Full Control – Easily manage your data through a simple interface.

Thoughtfully Designed

  • Modern Interface – Elegant animations and smooth navigation.
  • Dark Mode Support – Comfortable viewing in any lighting condition.
  • Accessibility – Designed to work seamlessly for all users.
