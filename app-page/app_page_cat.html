Cat Breed Identifier - App Details

App Overview

Cat Breed Identifier is your smart, AI-powered companion for identifying cat breeds from photos. Instantly discover breed information, care recommendations, health insights, and personalized advice using advanced AI technology and comprehensive feline breed databases.

Core Identification Features

  • Camera Integration – Capture or upload photos for instant breed analysis.
  • Multi-Pass AI Analysis – First classifies size/shape, then specific breed for enhanced accuracy.
  • Confidence Scoring – 0.0-1.0 reliability scale with specific thresholds for result accuracy.
  • Smart Photo Tips – Guidance for optimal photo capture and lighting.
  • Mixed Breed Detection – Identifies primary and secondary breeds in mixed cats.

Breed Information & Insights

  • Comprehensive Breed Profiles – Detailed information on temperament, characteristics, and history.
  • Care Recommendations – Personalized advice for grooming, nutrition, and environmental needs.
  • Health Insights – Common health concerns and preventive care tips specific to each breed.
  • Temperament Analysis – Understanding your cat's personality traits and behavioral patterns.

Premium Features

  • Unlimited Scans – No daily limits on breed identification.

Free users get 1 scan per day with basic breed information.

Scan History & Management

  • Local Scan History – Securely saved on your device with SwiftData.
  • Search & Filter – Easily find previous scans by breed or date.
  • Export Options – Share results with veterinarians or family.

User Experience

  • Camera-First Design – Optimized for quick and easy photo capture.
  • Onboarding Flow – Guided introduction to app features and camera permissions.
  • Multi-Language Support – Available in English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Ukrainian, and Chinese.
  • Modern SwiftUI Interface – Clean, intuitive design optimized for iOS 17.6+.
  • Quick Actions – iOS shortcuts integration for faster access to scanning and feedback.

Technical Features

  • AI-Powered – OpenAI integration via AIProxy service for accurate breed detection.
  • Privacy-First – All data stored locally, images processed securely.
  • Camera Permissions – Smart permission handling with educational prompts.
  • RevenueCat Integration – Seamless subscription management with remote paywall configuration.

Feedback & Support

  • Feature Requests – WishKit integration for community-driven development.
  • Email Support – Built-in mail composition for user assistance.
  • Review Prompts – Smart timing for App Store review requests.
  • Accuracy Feedback – Help improve AI accuracy with result feedback.
